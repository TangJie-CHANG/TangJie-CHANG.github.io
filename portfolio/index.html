Research about notification speculation

National Chiao-Tung University

Oct 2019 – Present Hsinchu, Taiwan
We will be afraid of missing important notifications if we choose not to look the notification, but if we choose to see it and find out that it is not important, this may waste our time and even cause interrupt. According to published paper, many users tend to guess the source of notification before looking at it. So my research is on helping user to guess notification’s source.

My first collaborate paper!

A Conversation Analysis of Non-Progress and Coping Strategies with a Banking Task-Oriented Chatbot

Jul 2019 – Dec 2019 CHI 2020
I took over the research about Chatbot (which was published as poster in MobileHCI with preliminary results), I was responsible for data analysis to make more deeper results. We found that there are 12 types of conversational NP (Non-progress, which means the conversation can’t continue.) and 10 types of coping strategies (user cope with the NP). Also found three signs that user may leave the chatbot forever. Recommendations were provided to help developer design chatbot to make user have better experience using it.
